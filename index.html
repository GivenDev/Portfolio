About Me

I'm Given Monaise, an HTML Email Developer based in Johannesburg. I specialise in the design, development and testing of responsive and accessible HTML emails.

Personal development is important to me and I'm constantly improving technical knowledge and skills to get the best results from every campaign. My ability to work within tight deadlines and attention to detail ensures that I deliver projects on time, on budget and on specification.
